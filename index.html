Data Can Be Beautiful

Create a compelling data story by designing a report that is both visually appealing and easy to understand.

SAS Visual Analytics report builders asked for guidance on how to create effective reports. In response, we created this site for you. We created this site with the following four principles in mind.

Keep it Simple

Data visualization tools are more powerful than ever and allow you to create almost anything from a data set.Follow the design principle of “less is more.” Clarity always outweighs complexity in report creation. Overwhelming your report audience with too much information, too many visuals, or too many sections can cause them to lose focus. Cut down wherever possible: don’t say in 500 words what you can say in 4. Don’t create a 12 section report when you can get to the same insights in 2.

Help Your Users

Establish your data story, stick to that story, and create a report which enhances that story. Report viewers want the highlights – concerns in the data, action items, etc. Attention is limited. Make effective choices when choosing visualizations and laying out your page so your users can find the insights they need. Memory is limited. Remind your users of necessary actions from page to page. Do not expect your report viewers to remember key insights.

Accessibility

Creating an accessible report does not only refer to individuals with special needs. Your report needs to be accessible for new users, older users, etc. Knowing your audience can help in your report creation. Anticipating their needs and understanding their limitations can help inform your report and ensure everyone has the ability to gain insight from your data story.Understanding basic concepts of accessible content creation (adequate labeling, color usage, etc.) can empower your own decision making as you create reports for your company.
